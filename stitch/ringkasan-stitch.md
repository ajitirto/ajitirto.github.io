
# Project Brief & PRD: Aji Tirto Prayogo Personal Landing Page

## 1. Project Overview
A high-fidelity, minimalist personal landing page designed to showcase the professional identity and social presence of Aji Tirto Prayogo. The design focuses on a "Professional-First" strategy, clearly segmenting engineering credentials from social content.

## 2. Target Audience
*   Technical Recruiters & Engineering Managers
*   Open Source Collaborators
*   Conference Organizers
*   Professional Network

## 3. Design Strategy (Ethereal Professional)
*   **Visual Direction:** Modern Dark Mode with a crisp, high-contrast aesthetic.
*   **Glassmorphism:** Subtle use of background blurs and border-strokes to create depth without clutter.
*   **Typography:** Manrope — chosen for its clean, geometric, and modern feel.
*   **Color Palette:** Deep blacks (`#050505`) and dark grays (`#131313`) paired with white accents and varying levels of transparency for visual hierarchy.

## 4. Key Features & Functionality
### A. Thematic Versatility
*   **Dark/Light Mode Toggle:** A persistent UI component in the header allowing users to switch themes.
*   **Adaptive Styling:** Design tokens (glassmorphism, shadows) automatically adjust for readability and aesthetic consistency in both modes.

### B. Content Segmentation
*   **Professional Identity (Primary):** High-impact cards for LinkedIn, GitHub, and GitLab.
*   **Presentations List:** A structured template for speaking engagements, including title, event name, and date.
*   **Social Presence (Secondary):** A minimalist, icon-only footer section for X (Twitter), Instagram, and Blog/Writing.

### C. Direct Integrations
*   **Blog:** Direct routing to `blog.ajitirto.my.id`.
*   **CV:** Primary CTA for resume/CV download in the global navigation.

## 5. Technical Specifications
*   **Framework:** HTML5 / Tailwind CSS
*   **Interactivity:** 300ms transitions on all interactive elements.
*   **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first architecture, scaling up to ultra-wide desktop views.
*   **Animation:** `hover:scale-105` and `hover:brightness-125` for tactile feedback.

## 6. Information Architecture
1.  **Header:** Logo, Navigation Links, Theme Toggle, Download CV CTA.
2.  **Hero:** Profile Image, Name, Headline, Short Bio.
3.  **Professional Identity:** 3-column grid (Desktop) of core professional platforms.
4.  **Presentations:** Vertical list of templated entries.
5.  **Social Media:** Horizontal row of minimalist icons.
6.  **Footer:** Copyright and secondary links.
